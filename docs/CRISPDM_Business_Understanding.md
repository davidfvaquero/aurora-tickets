# Business Understanding

## Problem statement

Aurora Tickets has high traffic but weak conversion, sporadic latency and error spikes, and signals of suspicious bot traffic. The project objective is to create an AWS-based observability and analytics platform that connects clickstream behavior with commercial outcomes.

## Stakeholders

- Product and growth teams: need conversion and campaign insight.
- Operations team: needs errors and latency visibility.
- Management: needs event ranking by interest and revenue.

## KPIs

- Funnel conversion rate per day.
- Revenue per event and event-level interest-to-purchase ratio.
- Number of suspicious IP/page combinations detected per day.

## Decisions enabled

- Which events deserve more promotion.
- Which landing pages or endpoints need performance fixes.
- Which traffic sources should be blocked, rate-limited, or investigated.
